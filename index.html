Office Location
🚃
Directions
1
Tokyo Metro
Hibiya Line – Roppongi Station (Exit 4)

Take Exit 4 and walk approximately 8 minutes toward Midtown Tower.

2
Toei Subway
Oedo Line – Roppongi Station (Exit 7)

Take Exit 7 and walk approximately 5 minutes. Head toward the main entrance of Tokyo Midtown.

3
Arriving at the Building
Enter Midtown Tower

At the main lobby, please inform the front desk staff that you are visiting "WEBTOON Japan on the 11th Floor." Register as a visitor, then take the elevator to Floor 11.

4
At Floor 11
Check in at the Reception Desk

Please wait at the reception and your host will come to greet and escort you.
